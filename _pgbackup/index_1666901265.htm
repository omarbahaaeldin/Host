Creation Process

To create trust in your company, it's important that your graphic design assets align with your company's voice. I help you communicate with your customers using graphic design to capture the viewer's attention and convey your brand values and message using visual elements. If you are looking for a unique design style for your company and product, here is my design process to help you connect with your customers.

1

Connect
The first step in my design process for Graphic Design is to connect with you and know what are your needs.

2

Define Ideas
We will define your ideas with a design brief, to make clear your objectives with your graphic design project.

3

Research
I start the process of Research, collecting useful information, ideas, and inspiration to create a mood board that later will use to develop the design.

4

 Brainstorming
Once I already have all the information from the mood board ready I start to brainstorm ideas and trying the first raw ideas that later I will develop.

5

Development
Once I made the first raw test with design I choose the best options and start developing these ideas into the final design that later will present you.

6

Improving Details
The last part of my design process is adjusting details of the graphic design project after your feedback to deliver you the best result.
